Our Mission

Sovereignty in an
AI-Driven World.

At SOWA Privacy, we believe that data protection is not an obstacle to innovation, but should be its foundation.

The Challenge

In today's digital landscape, companies face a dilemma: either utilize the immense productivity of modern AI systems while risking the leakage of sensitive data, or forgo progress entirely for compliance reasons.

This is where we come in. SOWA Privacy is not just a tool; it is a paradigm shift in handling artificial intelligence within business-critical infrastructures.

100% Local.
100% Secure.

Our technology is based on the principle of local data sovereignty. Not a single piece of information leaves your device in plain text.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every organization – whether a government agency, hospital, or industrial conglomerate – can use AI models like ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude with absolute confidence.

SOWA Privacy acts as a technological shield that recognizes what is worth protecting in real-time and anonymizes it before the cloud ever finds out.
